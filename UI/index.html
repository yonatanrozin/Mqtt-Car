Welcome to Yonatan Rozin's wireless car controller!

This controller allows users to control the car wirelessly for up to 30 seconds on a first-come-first-served basis. After 30 seconds, the controls will be automatically transfered to the next user in the queue. Once your turn has ended, feel free to refresh the page for another turn. Note that this controller does NOT work while connected to the NYU WiFi network.

To join the queue, press the Connect to MQTT button above!
